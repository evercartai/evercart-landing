FAQ

Honest answers.

Does it really run without me involved day-to-day? +
Yes. Once your store is live the six agents continue running autonomously. The Optimizer monitors ROAS and shifts budgets daily, the Support agent handles every customer message, and the Marketer agent refreshes creatives based on live trend data. You can monitor everything from the dashboard. You only need to be involved when you choose to be.
What happens if the agents make a mistake? +
Every agent output is logged, traceable, and reviewable. The agents are designed to fail gracefully. If a Shopify mutation fails, the system logs the error and continues with the remaining tasks rather than crashing entirely. Founding members also get direct access to Doug for anything that does not work as expected.
Is my store actually on Shopify? +
Yes. The Coder agent builds a real Shopify store on your account using Shopify's Admin GraphQL API. You own it. You can log in, make changes, and keep it running even if you ever stop using EverCart AI. You can see a live example at velvet-skin-24.myshopify.com.
What is the revenue share and how does it work? +
Founding members pay $49/mo and a 15% revenue share on store GMV. The revenue share is calculated on gross sales, not profit. It is deducted automatically via Stripe. We structured it this way deliberately. EverCart AI only earns when you earn, which means the agents are always optimizing for your revenue, not just engagement.
Can I cancel? +
Yes. Cancel any time from your dashboard with no penalty. Your Shopify store stays live and yours regardless. The agents stop running on your store when you cancel but everything they built remains.
